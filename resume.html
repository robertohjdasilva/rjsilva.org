Professional Summary

Principal Software Engineer with more than two decades of experience designing large-scale distributed systems across networking, virtualization, and cloud infrastructure.

My work focuses on distributed systems architecture, observability platforms, telemetry systems, and diagnostics frameworks used to understand, stabilize, and improve reliability in complex production environments.

At Microsoft, I lead the design of observability and diagnostics infrastructure for a distributed VM orchestration platform used by large-scale CI/CD systems, combining distributed tracing, telemetry pipelines, and automated diagnostics to help engineers detect failures, understand system behavior, and resolve incidents across thousands of distributed components.

Beyond engineering, I write about the human side of technology. I am the author of Impossible Crossing and the creator of Engineering with a Heart.
