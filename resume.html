Overview

Professional summary

Principal Software Engineer with 20+ years of experience designing large-scale distributed systems, networking software, observability platforms, and diagnostic infrastructure for enterprise environments. My work spans Ethernet switching, IP networking, remote access, virtualization, cloud platforms, telemetry systems, and distributed tracing.

Across my career I have contributed as developer, architect, debugger, systems thinker, and technical writer — combining deep systems programming with architectural leadership focused on reliability, visibility, performance, and operational transparency.

In parallel with engineering, I write about resilience, technology, and the human side of building systems. I am the author of Impossible Crossing and the voice behind Engineering with a Heart.
