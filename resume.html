Redmond, Washington

  • Delivered major performance gains, reducing ingestion and indexing time for large trace datasets from about 40 minutes to under 10 minutes, and historical comparison latency from about 2 minutes to under 5 seconds.
  • Improved VM infrastructure reliability by enhancing deployment pipelines, workload distribution across CPU cores, cgroup and namespace isolation, and VM lifecycle stability.
