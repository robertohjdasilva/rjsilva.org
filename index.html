About R.J. Silva

R.J. Silva is a Brazilian-born engineer, inventor, teacher, and storyteller. His work spans distributed systems, networking, observability, and software architecture, while his writing explores technology, humanity, and service.

He lived in Brazil, Europe, and the United States, and those crossings deeply shape the perspective behind Engineering with a Heart and Impossible Crossing.

“The most beautiful systems are invisible. When they work, people don’t think about them — they simply live better because of them.”
