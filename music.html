When the Memoir Continues in Sound

Music has always been more than a backdrop in my life. In many moments, it was refuge, company, strength, and sometimes the only way to understand what I was feeling before words were ready. Throughout this story, music stayed close — on the radio, in my headphones, in the lyrics that followed me while I studied, dreamed, suffered, fell in love, and tried to find my place in the world.

Some songs made me laugh. Others made me cry. Some gave me strength to prepare for the ITA entrance exam, some inspired me when everything felt difficult, and some always brought me back to the love of my life — the girl of my dreams. This book tells my story in words, but some emotions overflow language. That is why these two playlists matter so much to me: each one carries a chapter, a feeling, a memory, and a part of the person I became.

Playlist 1 — The Emotional Soundtrack of the Crossing

This playlist brings together the songs that accompanied me throughout the story told in this book. These are songs that touched my life in moments of joy, sadness, doubt, resilience, and love. They helped me study, dream higher, cross difficult seasons, and celebrate both small and great victories.

Here are songs that made me laugh, cry, and believe. They marked important phases of my life and still awaken very specific emotions. This is the emotional soundtrack of my journey.

Playlist 2 — The Night When Anyone Can Step Onto the Stage

This playlist was born from one unforgettable night: singing with the Kayak band at the CTA June festival, in front of hundreds of people, on an improvised stage that turned a distant dream into something real. It became proof that music also belongs to courage, joy, and human expression.

That night taught me something simple and powerful: many people carry music inside them, but fear comparison, judgment, or ridicule. This playlist keeps the sound of that night and the meaning it still carries for me.
